Muhammad Ali

"The Greatest"

Muhammad Ali was a professional boxer who was the best in the ring at the time. Ali was stripped of his title as he refused to go to the army in the Vietnam War. Ali almost gave up his whole career of boxing to protest in what he believed.

Quotes

"Float like a butterfly, sting like a bee."

"I hated every minute of training, but I said, 'Don't quit. Suffer now and live the rest of your life as a champion."

"Don't count the days, make the days count."
